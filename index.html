Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/02/2026

Labourly Pty Ltd (“Labourly”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Labourly mobile application and related services for workforce management and labour hire businesses in Australia.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide workforce management and payroll-related services.

Identity and Contact Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Residential address
  • Date of birth
  • Profile photograph (staff accounts)

Employment and Workforce Information

  • Shift scheduling data
  • Timesheet records
  • Employment contract signatures
  • Licences and certifications
  • Incident reports
  • Internal notes and comments

Financial and Sensitive Information

  • Bank account details
  • Superannuation details
  • Tax File Number (TFN)
  • Basic medical and health information (for workplace safety and emergency purposes)

Company Information

  • Company name and ABN
  • Company address
  • Authorised contact details

Media and Uploaded Content

The App may access your device camera to allow photographs to be taken within the App. Uploaded images and documents are securely stored in Australia.

2. Analytics and Session Replay

We use analytics services, including PostHog, to improve reliability and usability. Session replay is configured to obscure input fields and uploaded images. We do not use analytics for advertising.

3. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide scheduling and timesheet functionality
  • Process payroll and employment records
  • Send operational notifications
  • Comply with Australian legal obligations and workplace safety standards

4. Data Security

We implement encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), encryption at rest through managed cloud infrastructure, and access controls to protect personal information.

5. Data Retention

Certain payroll, health, and employment records may be retained for up to seven (7) years or longer as required under the Fair Work Act and Australian tax law.

6. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion within the App. Approved requests result in the deletion or anonymisation of personal account data, subject to our legal retention obligations.

If you cannot access the App and wish to request deletion via the web, please contact us from your registered email address at: info@labourly.com.au
