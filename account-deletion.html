Labourly Account Deletion Request

Labourly Pty Ltd allows users to request deletion of their Labourly account and associated personal information.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email info@labourly.com.au
  2. Include your full name and the email address associated with your account
  3. Confirm that you are requesting account deletion

What Happens Next

Once verified, your account will be deactivated and access to the app will be removed.

Data Retention

As Labourly is an employer and labour hire provider, certain employment, payroll, tax and workplace safety records must be retained for at least seven (7) years under Australian law.

All retained data is securely stored and not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions, email info@labourly.com.au
